App Features

The app can launch other Android apps while they're running, so you can multitask like you would on a real computer, for example, to write an email and browse emails at the same time, or have two notebooks open at the same time. This is even easier with the Android split screen function.

Simplicity

Just start the app and select the app to start twice

Work on the same Dataset

Fast

Because you can choose favourites you can start your apps very fast

Shortcuts

No need to clone

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I use DoubleLauncher with every app?

    No you can't. Apps can prevent beeing Double-Started.

  • Does DoubleLauncher modify the apps you use with it?

    No. DoubleLauncher does not modifys apps used with, but some apps dont manage being used with DoubleLauncher and behave in ways you can't predict.

  • Where can I download this app?

    You can download this app on Google Play Store

  • How can I add apps as shortcuts to the Homescreen

    You have to add the App you want on the Homescreen to ypur Top Apps, then you will get asked where you want this App on your Homescreen.

  • How can I add apps to my Top Apps?

    You have to prees it long, then you will be asked if you want to add it.

  • How can I double launch apps?

    1.Launch your App normal
    2.Open DoubleLauncher and search your app there
    3.Click it.

  • Why do I have to start my app normal and then to start it with double launcher?

    Usually, you don't realize that you need the app twice until it's already running. If you would start the app twice now, you would have the app three times, which would be very unpratic, because you only need the app twice.

  • How can I triple launch apps?

    1.Double launch your App
    2.Open DoubleLauncher and search your app there
    3.Click it.
