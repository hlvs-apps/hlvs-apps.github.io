How to launch Android apps twice without cloning them

  1. Home
  2. Tutorials
  3. How to DoubleLaunch

We have here a simple step-by-step guide on how to launch Android apps twice without cloning them:

  1. Launch the app you want to double launch.
  2. Download the app DoubleLauncher free from the Google-Playstore
  3. Launch the app
  4. Confirm that you have read through the terms and conditions
  5. Press the magnifying glass and search for the app you want to double launch
  6. The app will appear once again
  7. You can now switch between the two windows of the app you double launched by acting as if you wanted to switch between two apps. How this works depends on the device. On most devices there is a button (it can also be virtual) at the bottom of the screen.
